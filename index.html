About Forge Fit

Welcome to Forge Fit, where strength is not just built but forged. We believe that fitness is more than a routine—it’s a lifestyle. At Forge Fit, we are dedicated to helping you shape not only your body but also your mind and spirit, forging a path to a healthier, stronger you.

Founded on the principles of dedication, resilience, and community, Forge Fit offers a comprehensive approach to fitness that caters to all levels. Whether you’re just beginning your fitness journey or looking to take your training to the next level, our expert trainers and state-of-the-art facilities are here to guide and support you every step of the way.

Comfortable, athletic wear is ideal for all classes. For yoga and Pilates, stretchy clothing that allows for a full range of motion is recommended. Don’t forget to wear supportive athletic shoes for classes like Bootcamp, CrossFit, and Kickboxing, while bare feet or grip socks are acceptable for Yoga and Pilates.

With our standard membership, you can attend an unlimited number of group classes per week! This allows you to mix and match different programs to keep your workouts varied and exciting. However, some specialized classes may have limited spots, so it’s a good idea to reserve your spot in advance.

If you miss a class, don’t worry! Our classes are designed to be welcoming, and you can always join the next available session. You can also reach out to the instructor if you’d like to get a quick recap or modifications for what you missed.
